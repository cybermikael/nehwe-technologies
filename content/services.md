## NIST 800-171 Readiness Assessment
**What it is:**
A thourough evaluation of your current cybersecurity posture against NIST 800-171 controls and CMMC requirements.

**Deliverables**
- Gap analysis identifying missing controls
- Risk register (basic)
- Executive-ready summary
- 60-90 day remediation roadmap

**Time to complete:** 10 busiuness days
**Price:** $2,500 (flat, paid upfront)

## Plan of Action & Milestones (POA&M) Development

**What it is:**
A clear, actionable plan to remediate compliance gaps identified in your assessment.

**Deliverables:**
- Structured POA&M document
- Prioritized list of actions
- Assignable responsibilities and timelines

**Why it's valuable"**
Auditors and management need to see a realistic, achievable roadmap - this helps you stay on track and demonstrate progress.

## Policy & Procedure Documentation

**What it is:**
Creation or update of your security policies, aligned with NIST 800-171 controls.

**Deliverables:**
- Password policies
- Access control policies
- Incident response procedures
- Backup and data handling procedures

**Benefit:**
Small contractors often lack documented policies - this is a compliance blocker. Having these in place ensures audit-readiness.
